How to use

PROF10 has the goal to help improving college education throughout consistent feedbacks to professors and TAs. It is permanently prohibited to insult or disrespect the profession of professors. Those who do not follow the rules, will be permanently banned. You will evaluate your professor in some different dimensios. Difficulty will not count for professor's general rating. It is only an informative dimension. The scale used is:
  • 0

    The professor is really bad in that dimension, that no other can balance and he should not teach unless he improves those skills.

  • 1

    The professor has serious problems with this dimension and he shoul urgently tries to improve it.

  • 2

    The professor is bad in this dimension.

  • 3

    The professor is ok with this dimension, but not great. He can still improves it.

  • 4

    The professor is pretty good in this dimension and the students should not worry, because he will do a good job.

  • 5

    The professor aces in this dimension. He is great and has nothing to improve at all. Go have your class!

The other dimensions are:
  • Teaching
    How is the quality of the class? Does the professor passes the knowledge clearly?
  • Material
    How is the class material? Does the professor provide good handover and have things organized?
  • Participation
    Does de professor care about the students? Does he attend office hours?
  • Attendance
    Is attendance mandatory or not? Will you still do a good job without attending classes?
